No Bake Chocolate Covered Krispy Bars


Ingredients


 - 6 cup of rice, white, long-grain, regular, unenriched, cooked without salt


 - 10 1/2 ounce of candies, marshmallows


 - 3 tablespoon of peanut butter, smooth style, without salt


 - 6 ounce of spartan, real semi-sweet chocolate baking chips, upc: 011213162966


 - 14 cup of margarine, regular, 80% fat, composite, stick, without salt


Instructions: 

microwave marshmallows & margarine for 2-3 minutes in large bowl. add peanut butter , mix thoroughly. press into oil sprayed dish. melt chocolate chips, mix well then spread on top. Cover & cool till serving.
