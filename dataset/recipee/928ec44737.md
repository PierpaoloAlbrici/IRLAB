## Strawberry Lemonade Quencher

[928ec44737](http://www.food.com/recipe/strawberry-lemonade-quencher-242371)

### Ingredients

 - 1 cup of lemon juice, raw

 - 10 ounce of strawberries, raw

 - 10 cup of water, bottled, generic

### Instructions

Thaw frozen daiquiri mix. Blend together Strawberry Daiquiri Mix and lemon juice in a two-quart pitcher. Mix with water. Garnish with fresh sliced strawberries and lemons. To make a quick 2nd batch: Pre-mix together all ingredients except water and refrigerate.
