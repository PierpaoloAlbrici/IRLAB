Grilled Basil and Lemon Pepper Chicken


Ingredients


 - 1 tablespoon of sugars, brown


 - 1 teaspoon of spices, pepper, black


 - 1 teaspoon of spices, basil, dried


 - 1/2 teaspoon of spices, garlic powder


 - 1/2 teaspoon of salt, table


 - 1 pound of chicken, broiler or fryers, breast, skinless, boneless, meat only, raw


Instructions: 

Mix all ingredients except chicken breast together. Spread rub evenly over chicken. Cover and refrigerate for 15 minutes. Grill chicken until done.
