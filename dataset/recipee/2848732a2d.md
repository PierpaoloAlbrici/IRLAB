## Beer Rice Recipe

[2848732a2d](http://cookeatshare.com/recipes/beer-rice-83009)

### Ingredients

 - 1 cup of rice, white, long-grain, regular, unenriched, cooked without salt

 - 1 cup of alcoholic beverage, beer, regular, all

 - 1 1/4 cup of soup, chicken broth or bouillon, dry

 - 1 tablespoon of butter, without salt

 - 1/4 cup of oil, corn, peanut, and olive

 - 1/2 teaspoon of spices, pepper, black

### Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Place all ingredients in a 2 1/2-qt casserole dish lightly coated with cooking spray or possibly oil. Cover and bake till all liquid is absorbed, about 1 1/4 hrs. Yield: 4 servings.
