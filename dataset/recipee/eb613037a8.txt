 - 16 ounce of cheese, parmesan, hard


 - 12 cup of cream, sour, cultured

 - 18 teaspoon of sauce, ready-to-serve, pepper, tabasco


 - 12 teaspoon of spices, parsley, dried


 - 14 teaspoon of spices, thyme, dried

Instructions:
