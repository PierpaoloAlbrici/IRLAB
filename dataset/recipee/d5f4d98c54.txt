Blueberry Sorbet


Ingredients


 - 1 pound of blueberries, raw


 - 12 cup of yogurt, greek, plain, nonfat


 - 2 tablespoon of sugars, granulated


 - 3 tablespoon of water, bottled, generic


Instructions: 

Combine all ingredients in food processor. Process until pureed. Enjoy right away or freeze for later.
