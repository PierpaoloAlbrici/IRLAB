 - 1 cup of milk, buttermilk, fluid, cultured, lowfat

 - 2 tablespoon of sugars, granulated

Place flour,, baking soda, salt in bread machine. Heat up buttermilk & butter to 110 degrees F. add yeast to buttermilk mixture.
