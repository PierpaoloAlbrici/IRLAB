## Heart-healthy, brain-boosting cholesterol-free Tofu Mayo

[d95027d250](http://www.food.com/recipe/heart-healthy-brain-boosting-cholesterol-free-tofu-mayo-71646)

### Ingredients

 - 5 ounce of tofu, raw, regular, prepared with calcium sulfate

 - 3 tablespoon of soymilk, original and vanilla, unfortified

 - 3 tablespoon of seeds, flaxseed

 - 1 tablespoon of mustard, prepared, yellow

 - 12 teaspoon of salt, table

### Instructions

Blend ingredients in a food processor for a full 1-2 minutes, or until it's smooth and creamy. Chill and use in place of traditional mayonnaise or salad dressing.
