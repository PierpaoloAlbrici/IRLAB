Cereal with Yogurt & Fruit


Ingredients


 - 1 cup of cereals, oats, regular and quick, unenriched, cooked with water (includes boiling and microwaving), without salt


 - 1 cup of yogurt, greek, plain, nonfat


 - 1/2 cup of peaches, yellow, raw


Instructions: 

Top cereal with yogurt and peach slices. Serve immediately.
