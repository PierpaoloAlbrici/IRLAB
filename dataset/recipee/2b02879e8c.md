### Ingredients

 - 2 tablespoon of salad dressing, mayonnaise, regular

 - 2 tablespoon of olives, ripe, canned (small-extra large)

 - 18 teaspoon of spices, garlic powder

 - 1 dash of spices, thyme, dried
