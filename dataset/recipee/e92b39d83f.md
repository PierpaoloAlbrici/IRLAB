### Ingredients

 - 12 cup of yogurt, greek, plain, nonfat

 - 2 teaspoon of beef, grass-fed, ground, raw

 - 2 teaspoon of onions, raw

 - 12 teaspoon of sauce, worcestershire

### Instructions
