## Pina Colada

[57c2382314](http://www.food.com/recipe/pina-colada-187906)

### Ingredients

 - 3 cup of pineapple juice, canned or bottled, unsweetened, without added ascorbic acid

 - 3 cup of water, bottled, generic

 - 3-5 tablespoon of shortening confectionery, coconut (hydrogenated) and or palm kernel (hydrogenated)

 - 3 tablespoon of sugars, granulated

 - 3 tablespoon of pineapple, raw, all varieties

 - 3 tablespoon of cream, whipped, cream topping, pressurized

### Instructions

Simply blend together all the ingredients in a blender or smoothie maker. Serve chilled.
