Fast Low-Carb Hot Chocolate (Aztec Style)


Ingredients


 - 2 ounce of spartan, real semi-sweet chocolate baking chips, upc: 011213162966


 - 2 teaspoon of vanilla extract


 - 3 teaspoon of sugars, granulated


 - 12 cup of cream, fluid, heavy whipping


 - 34 cup of water, bottled, generic


 - 1 pinch of spices, pepper, red or cayenne


 - 1 pinch of sauce, peppers, hot, chili, mature red, canned


 - 1 pinch of spices, cinnamon, ground


Instructions: 

Melt the chocolate in the microwave about 2 minutes at 50% power. Stir in vanilla extract, Splenda, heavy whipping cream, cayenne, chili, and cinnamon. Add hot water and serve. Makes 1 delicious serving.
