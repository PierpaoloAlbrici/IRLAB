## Cranberry-Orange Mayonnaise

[313d46e768](http://www.foodgeeks.com/recipes/2849)

### Ingredients

 - 1/2 cup of salad dressing, mayonnaise, regular

 - 1/4 to 1/3 cup of cranberry sauce, canned, sweetened

 - 1/4 teaspoon of orange juice, raw

### Instructions

Stir all ingredients together. Serve or refrigerate.
