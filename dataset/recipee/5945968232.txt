4 Ingredient Sweet and Spicy Chicken Wings


Ingredients


 - 2 1/2-3 pound of chicken, broilers or fryers, wing, meat and skin, raw


 - 12 cup of honey


 - 12 cup of soy sauce made from soy (tamari)


 - 12 cup of sauce, barbecue


Instructions: 

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place wings in a greased 9 x 13 baking dish. Combine sauces and honey and pour over the wings. Bake for about an hour, or until juices run clear.
