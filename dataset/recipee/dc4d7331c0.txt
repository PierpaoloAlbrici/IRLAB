Beet Relish


Ingredients


 - 2.0 pound of beets, raw


 - 1/2 cup of vinegar, distilled


 - 1/4 cup of sugars, granulated


 - 1/2 teaspoon of salt, table


 - 5.0 ounce of horseradish, prepared


Instructions: 

Grate the beets and drain. Mix with vinegar, sugar, salt, and the horseradish. Sample as you go along for sweetness, and to see if it is strong enough.
