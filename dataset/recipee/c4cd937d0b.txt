Dry Stir-Fry Seasoning Mix


Ingredients


 - 14 cup of soup, chicken broth or bouillon, dry


 - 3 tablespoon of cornstarch


 - 2 tablespoon of sugars, granulated


 - 2 tablespoon of onions, raw


 - 2 tablespoon of spices, garlic powder


 - 2 teaspoon of spices, parsley, dried


 - 12 teaspoon of spices, ginger, ground


 - 14 teaspoon of peppers, sweet, green, raw


Instructions: 

In a small bowl, stir bouillon granules, cornstarch, sugar, onion, garlic, parsley, ginger and red pepper until blended. Store in an airtight container in a cool place. Stir mixture before measuring. Use like any dry stir-fry mix. 1 tablespoon equals one packet.
