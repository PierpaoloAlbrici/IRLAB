## Glorious Morning Cheesy Bacon Muffins

[64dbbe52a9](https://recipeland.com/recipe/v/glorious-morning-cheesy-bacon-m-44290)

### Ingredients

 - 1 cup of oats

 - 1 1/2 cup of wheat flour, white, all-purpose, unenriched

 - 1 1/4 cup of cheese, parmesan, hard

 - 3 tablespoon of sugars, granulated

 - 1 tablespoon of leavening agents, baking powder, double-acting, sodium aluminum sulfate

 - 1/2 teaspoon of spices, chili powder

 - 1/2 teaspoon of salt, table

 - 1/4 teaspoon of spices, pepper, black

 - 1/2 pound of bacon, meatless

 - 13 cup of oil, olive, salad or cooking

 - 1/4 cup of egg substitute, powder

 - 1 cup of milk, fluid, 1% fat, without added vitamin a and vitamin d

### Instructions

Preheat oven to 400F (200C). Grease muffin tin well. In large bowl combine oats, flour, sugar, baking powder and seasonings; set aside. In another bowl, combine oil, egg substitute and skim milk. Make a well in center of dry mixture. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients; stir until just moistened. Gently fold in bacon and cheese. Portion muffin batter evenly into prepared muffin tin (about 13 cup). Bake about 20 to 25 minutes or until lightly golden brown.
