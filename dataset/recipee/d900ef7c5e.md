### Ingredients

 - 1 1/2 pound of wheat flours, bread, unenriched

 - 1/2-1 pound of butter, without salt

### Instructions

Remove crusts, but bread into 1" cubes and place in a large bowl. Cut butter into small cubes; toss together with bread cubes.
