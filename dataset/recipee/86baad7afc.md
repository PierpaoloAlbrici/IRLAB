### Ingredients

 - 1 cup of cream, sour, cultured

 - 12 cup of cheese, parmesan, hard

 - 12 cup of cranberries, dried, sweetened

 - 1 teaspoon of horseradish, prepared

### Instructions

Combine all ingredients and chill well.
