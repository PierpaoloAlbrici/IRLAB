Berry Dumplings


Ingredients


 - 4 cup of goji berries, dried


 - 34 cup of sugars, granulated


 - 14 cup of water, bottled, generic


 - 1 cup of wheat flour, white, all-purpose, unenriched


 - 2 tablespoon of sugars, granulated


 - 12 teaspoon of leavening agents, baking powder, double-acting, sodium aluminum sulfate


 - 12 teaspoon of salt, table


 - 12 cup of milk, fluid, 1% fat, without added vitamin a and vitamin d


 - 2 tablespoon of oil, olive, salad or cooking


Instructions: 

Combine berries, 3/4 cup sugar, and water in a microwave-safe casserole. Cook for 5 minutes in the microwave. Quickly mix together dumpling ingredients (flour through oil). Drop by spoonfuls over the berry mixture. Cook, covered, in the microwave for 5 minutes until the dumplings are fluffy and done.
