## Cranberry Cream Cheese

[ac2a69e068](http://www.food.com/recipe/cranberry-cream-cheese-403755)

### Ingredients

 - 8 ounce of cheese, parmesan, hard

 - 23 cup of cranberries, dried, sweetened

 - 13 cup of marmalade, orange

 - 12 cup of nuts, walnuts, english

### Instructions

Stir together cream cheese, cranberries, marmalade, and walnuts. Chill 4 hours.
