## Fat Freddie's Beet Horseradish

[2bd431af2c](http://www.food.com/recipe/fat-freddies-beet-horseradish-95765)

### Ingredients

 - 1 pound of beets, raw

 - 1 teaspoon of salt, table

 - 2 ounce of horseradish, prepared

 - 12 cup of water, bottled, generic

 - 2 tablespoon of vinegar, distilled

 - 1 teaspoon of spices, pepper, black

 - 1 teaspoon of sugars, granulated

### Instructions

Grate beets and horseradish. Combine vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper and water. Bring to a boil. Pour over grated beets and horseradish. Mix thoroughly. Store in jar in refrigerator.
