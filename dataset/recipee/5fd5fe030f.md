## Tatortot Hotdish

[5fd5fe030f](https://recipeland.com/recipe/v/tatortot-hotdish-54179)

### Ingredients

 - 1 pound of beef, grass-fed, ground, raw

 - 2 ounce of soup, cream of chicken, canned, condensed

### Instructions

Cook hamburger. Pour into pan. Pour cream of mushroom soup over top. Put enough tatortots to cover. Cook at 350F (180C) for 30 minutes until finished.
