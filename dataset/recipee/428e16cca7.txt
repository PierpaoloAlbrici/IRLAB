Citrus Grove Punch


Ingredients


 - 3 cup of sugars, granulated


 - 2 cup of water, bottled, generic


 - 6 cup of orange juice, raw


 - 6 cup of grapefruit juice, white, raw


 - 1 1/2 cup of lime juice, raw


 - 1 liter of beverages, carbonated, ginger ale


Instructions: 

In a saucepan, bring sugar and water to a boil; cook for 5 minutes. Cover and refrigerate until cool. Combine juices and sugar mixture; mix well. Just before serving, stir in ginger ale. Serve over ice.
