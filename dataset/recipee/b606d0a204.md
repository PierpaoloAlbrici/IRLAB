## Iz's Simple Slaw

### Ingredients

 - 1 cup of cabbage, raw

 - 1/4 cup of carrots, raw

 - 1 tablespoon of oil, olive, salad or cooking

 - 1 tablespoon of vinegar, cider

 - 1/4 teaspoon of spices, garlic powder

### Instructions

Combine all ingredients into a bowl
