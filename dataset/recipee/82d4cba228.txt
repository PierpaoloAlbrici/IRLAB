Spicy Pico De Gallo


Ingredients


 - 1 1/2 cup of tomatoes, red, ripe, raw, year round average


 - 12 cup of onions, raw


 - 14 cup of spices, coriander seed


 - 1 tablespoon of peppers, jalapeno, raw


 - 14 cup of sauce, salsa, ready-to-serve


 - 1 tablespoon of lime juice, raw


Instructions: 

Combine tomatoes, onion, cilantro and jalapeno pepper in large bowl. Sir in picante sauce and lime juice. Cover; refrigerate 1 hour to let flavors blend.
