## Chocolate Ganache

[d011603e85](http://www.delish.com/recipefinder/chocolate-ganache-recipe)

### Ingredients

 - 1/4 cup of cream, fluid, heavy whipping

 - 4 ounce of candies, semisweet chocolate

### Instructions
