## Mom's Salad Dressing (Red Wine Vinegar)

[eff076eade](http://www.food.com/recipe/moms-salad-dressing-red-wine-vinegar-440175)

### Ingredients

 - 1 tablespoon of vinegar, red wine

 - 1 tablespoon of sugars, granulated

 - 3 tablespoon of salad dressing, mayonnaise, regular

### Instructions

Stir 'til smooth (I actually shake 'til smooth). Pour over simple salad of lettuce, avocado, tomato and green onions, toss to completely cover.
