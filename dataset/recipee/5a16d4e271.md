## Raw Rice and White Sesame Cookies

[5a16d4e271](https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/151758-raw-rice-and-white-sesame-cookies)

### Ingredients

 - 200 g of rice, white, long-grain, regular, unenriched, cooked without salt

 - 40 g of butter, without salt

 - 40 g of honey

 - 1 tablespoon of seeds, sesame seeds, whole, dried

### Instructions

Put all the ingredients in a bowl and mix them gently with a spatula. Use small ice cream scoop to shape the mixture into small cookies, and arrange them on a mesh rack. Dry them for 12 hours in a dehydrator.
