## Racy Raisin Rice Pudding

[518eacc1cc](http://www.food.com/recipe/racy-raisin-rice-pudding-26663)

### Ingredients

 - 2-3 cup of soymilk, original and vanilla, unfortified

 - 2 cup of rice, white, long-grain, regular, unenriched, cooked without salt

 - 1 1/2 teaspoon of spices, cinnamon, ground

 - 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract

 - 1 cup of raisins, seeded

 - 12 cup of nuts, almonds

 - 14 cup of sweetener, syrup, agave

### Instructions

In a medium saucepan, add the milk, cooked rice, cinnamon, vanilla, raisins, almonds, and sweetener and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer on low heat for 15-20 minutes until pudding thickens to desired consistancy, stirring occasionally.
