Chili Powder Blend


Ingredients


 - 3 tablespoon of spices, chili powder


 - 1 tablespoon of spices, cumin seed


 - 2 teaspoon of spices, oregano, dried


 - 2 teaspoon of spices, garlic powder


 - 1 teaspoon of spices, coriander seed


 - 1/2 teaspoon of spices, pepper, black


 - 1 teaspoon of salt, table


Instructions: 

Mix all of the above ingredients together. Store in an airtight jar. Use in any recipe that calls for chili powder.
