Plain Quinoa


Ingredients


 - 2 cup of water, bottled, generic


 - 1 cup of quinoa, uncooked


Instructions: 

Bring 2 cups water to a boil in 2 quart sauce pan. Add 1 cup Toasted Quinoa and return to boil, cover and cook over medium heat for 12 to 15 minutes or until Quinoa has absorbed all the water. Remove from heat and fluff, cover and let stand for 15 minutes.
