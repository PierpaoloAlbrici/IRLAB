Brie Mashed Potatoes


Ingredients


 - 1 1/2 pound of potatoes, raw, skin


 - 13 cup of cream, fluid, heavy whipping


 - 2 tablespoon of butter, without salt


 - 12 teaspoon of salt, table


 - 18 teaspoon of spices, pepper, black


 - 3 ounce of cheese, brie


 - 1 teaspoon of spices, thyme, dried


Instructions: 

Boil potatoes until tender about 25 to 30 minutes. Drain and mash potatoes. Add cream, butter, salt, pepper, cubed brie and thyme. Blend until smooth.
