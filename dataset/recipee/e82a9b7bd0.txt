Pecan Honey Butter


Ingredients


 - 1 1/2 cup of butter, without salt


 - 1 cup of oil, olive, salad or cooking


 - 12 cup of honey


 - 1 teaspoon of spices, cinnamon, ground


 - 1 cup of nuts, pecans


Instructions: 

Blend first 4 ingredients. Fold in pecans. Refrigerate in a airtight container.
