Rustic Cherry Apple Pie


Ingredients


 - 2 cup of wheat flour, white, all-purpose, unenriched


 - 2 tablespoon of sugars, powdered


 - 1 teaspoon of salt, table


 - 34 cup of butter, without salt


 - 1 teaspoon of vinegar, distilled


 - 5-6 tablespoon of water, bottled, generic


 - 12 cup of sugars, brown


 - 14 cup of cornstarch


 - 1 teaspoon of spices, cinnamon, ground


 - 3 cup of apples, raw, with skin


 - 1 cup of cherries, sweet, raw


 - 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, raw


 - 5 tablespoon of butter, without salt


 - 14 cup of wheat flour, white, all-purpose, unenriched


 - 14 cup of sugars, brown


 - 2 tablespoon of butter, without salt


Instructions: 

Pie Crust:. In a bowl, stir together flour, sugar and salt. Using a pastry blender, cut butter cubes into flour mixture until pieces are pea-size. Sprinkle vinegar over flour mixture, tossing with a fork. Sprinkle 5 to 6 tablespoons cold water, 1 tablespoon at a time, over flour mixture, tossing with a fork each time until dough is moistened and crumbly. Gather dough into a ball, flatten into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 4 hours. Roll dough into a 12-inch circle. Filling:. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. In a bowl, stir together sugar, cornstarch and cinnamon. Add apples to cinnamon sugar mixture and mix thoroughly. Add cherries and lemon juice and mix thoroughly. Place filling in center of crust, dot with butter. Bring pastry up and 2 to 3 inches over the filling, overlap or pleat and gather folds in the dough, gently press together where necessary. For Topping:. Stir together flour and brown sugar. Cut in butter until crumbly, sprinkle over apples and butter. Bake 40 to 50 minutes, until crust is golden. Remove to wire rack and cool.
