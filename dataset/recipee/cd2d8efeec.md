## Holiday Party Punch

[cd2d8efeec](http://www.food.com/recipe/holiday-party-punch-92169)

### Ingredients

 - 2 cup of water, bottled, generic

 - 34 cup of honey

 - 4 cup of cranberry juice, unsweetened

 - 2 cup of orange juice, raw

 - 1 cup of lemon juice, raw

 - 1 quart of beverages, carbonated, ginger ale

### Instructions

Combine boiling water and honey, stirring to dissolve. In a large punch bowl, combine cranberry, orange and lemon juices. Stir in the honey mixture. Just before serving, add the giner ale. Serve on ice with sliced fruit as garnish.
