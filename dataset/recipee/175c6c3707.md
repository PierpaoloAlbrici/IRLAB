## Cajun Seasoning Mix

[175c6c3707](http://www.food.com/recipe/cajun-seasoning-mix-199032)

### Ingredients

 - 4 teaspoon of salt, table

 - 4 teaspoon of spices, paprika

 - 3 teaspoon of spices, garlic powder

 - 3 teaspoon of spices, pepper, black

 - 2 1/2 teaspoon of spices, onion powder

 - 1 1/4 teaspoon of spices, pepper, red or cayenne

 - 1 1/2 teaspoon of spices, thyme, dried

 - 1 1/2 teaspoon of spices, oregano, dried

### Instructions

Combine all ingredients in small bowl and mix thoroughly Store in Air Tight Container or Spice Jar.
