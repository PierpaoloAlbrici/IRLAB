Soy Balsamic Ginger Sauce


Ingredients


 - 14 cup of soy sauce made from soy (tamari)


 - 14 cup of vinegar, balsamic


 - 2 tablespoon of water, bottled, generic


 - 1 tablespoon of spices, ginger, ground


Instructions: 

Combine all ingredients thoroughly. Spoon over baby bok choy or any other food that seems appealing.
