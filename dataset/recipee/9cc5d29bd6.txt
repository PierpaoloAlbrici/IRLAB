Gorgonzola Dip


Ingredients


 - 1 cup of il villaggio, mascarpone cheese, upc: 037982981300


 - 1/2 cup of cream, sour, cultured


 - 13 cup of chives, raw


 - 1/2 teaspoon of salt, table


 - 1/4 teaspoon of spices, pepper, black


 - 1 cup of cheese, parmesan, hard


Instructions: 

Mix mascarpone cheese and sour cream together until creamy. Stir in salt, pepper and chopped chives and mix thoroughly. Add cumbled gorgonzola and stir lightly, leaving dip to be chunky. Serve chilled with fresh apple and pear slices, crackers and wine. Lots and lots of wine.
