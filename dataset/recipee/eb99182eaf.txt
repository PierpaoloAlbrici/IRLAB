Chocolate Cake Truffles


Ingredients


 - 150 g of spartan, real semi-sweet chocolate baking chips, upc: 011213162966


 - 500 g of butter, without salt


 - 2 tablespoon of cream, whipped, cream topping, pressurized


 - 14 cup of sugars, powdered


 - 2 teaspoon of alcoholic beverage, distilled, rum, 80 proof


 - 13 cup of cocoa, dry powder, unsweetened


 - 14 cup of spartan, real semi-sweet chocolate baking chips, upc: 011213162966


Instructions: 

Combine the chocolate, butter, cream, sifted icing sugar and rum in a small pan. Stir over low heat until chocolate and butter have melted; remove from heat. Transfer mixture to medium mixing bowl. Refrigerate for 15 minutes or until semi-set. Using electric beaters, beat the mixture until it is creamy. Roll heaped teaspoonfuls combined cocoa and drinking chocolate until coated. Refrigerate until firm.
