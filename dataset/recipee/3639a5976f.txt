Spinach-and-Watermelon Salad


Ingredients


 - 1 tablespoon of honey


 - 1 tablespoon of vinegar, balsamic


 - 1 tablespoon of water, bottled, generic


 - 1 1/2 teaspoon of oil, olive, salad or cooking


 - 1/2 teaspoon of lemon juice, raw


 - 1/8 teaspoon of spices, tarragon, dried


 - 1 dash of salt, table


 - 4 cup of spinach, raw


 - 4 cup of lettuce, cos or romaine, raw


 - 2 cup of watermelon, raw


 - 1 cup of strawberries, raw


 - 1/2 cup of pickles, cucumber, sour


 - 1/4 cup of onions, raw


Instructions: 

Combine first 7 ingredients in a bowl; stir well with a whisk. Set aside.Combine spinach and remaining ingredients in a large bowl. Drizzle dressing over salad, and toss gently to coat. Serve immediately.
