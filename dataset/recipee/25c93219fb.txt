English Toffee


Ingredients


 - 1 cup of butter, salted


 - 1 cup of sugars, granulated


 - 23 cup of nuts, pecans


 - 1/2 cup of spartan, real semi-sweet chocolate baking chips, upc: 011213162966


Instructions: 

1. Put butter and sugar in a heavy saucepan and stir CONSTANTLY until golden brown (or the color of peanut butter). This should take anywhere from 10 to 25 minutes depending on what type of pan/skillet you use. Keep it on medium to medium-high heat. 2. Pour over chopped pecans in a 9 x 9 pan. DO NOT BUTTER THE PAN. 3. Put chocolate chips on top, while hot. Melt chips and spread. 4. Let it sit for 1/2 hour, then refrigerate for 1 hour. Cut (or break) into pieces.
