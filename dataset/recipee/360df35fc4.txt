Ginger-Sesame Vinaigrette


Ingredients


 - 12 cup of roland, seasoned rice wine vinegar, upc: 041224705142


 - 14 cup of water, bottled, generic


 - 14 cup of miso


 - 14 cup of onions, spring or scallions (includes tops and bulb), raw


 - 2 tablespoon of sugars, granulated


 - 2 tablespoon of spices, ginger, ground


 - 2 tablespoon of soy sauce made from soy (tamari)


 - 4 teaspoon of oil, canola


 - 2 teaspoon of oil, sesame, salad or cooking


Instructions: 

Combine first 3 ingredients in a medium bowl, stirring with a whisk until smooth. Stir in green onions and the remaining ingredients.
