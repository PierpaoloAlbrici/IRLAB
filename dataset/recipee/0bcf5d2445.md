### Ingredients

 - 23 cup of salad dressing, mayonnaise, regular

 - 1 teaspoon of parsley, fresh

 - 1 teaspoon of dill weed, fresh

 - 1 dash of spices, garlic powder

 - 23 cup of cream, sour, cultured
