## Gin Buck

[7082094d10](http://www.food.com/recipe/gin-buck-330100)

### Ingredients

 - 1 1/2 ounce of alcoholic beverage, distilled, gin, 90 proof

 - 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, raw

 - 6 ounce of beverages, carbonated, ginger ale

### Instructions

Mix gin and lemon juice in a shaker. Pour over ice cubes in an old fashioned glass. Top with gingerale.
