Ingredients


 - 300 g of wheat flour, white, all-purpose, unenriched

 - 15 g of sugars, granulated


 - 4 g of salt, table


 - 4 4/5 g of leavening agents, yeast, baker's, active dry


 - 10 g of oil, olive, salad or cooking
