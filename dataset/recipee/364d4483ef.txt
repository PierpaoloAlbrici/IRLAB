Pumpkin Spice Steamer


Ingredients


 - 34 ounce of pumpkin, raw


 - 34 ounce of syrup, maple, canadian


 - 8 ounce of milk, fluid, 1% fat, without added vitamin a and vitamin d


Instructions: 

Steam syrups and cold milk together. Top with whipped cream and dust with pumpkin pie spice or nutmeg.
