Flavorful and Colorful Coleslaw


Ingredients


 - 1 cup of asparagus, raw


 - 1 cup of peas, green, frozen, unprepared


 - 1/2 cup of water, bottled, generic


 - 6 cup of cabbage, raw


 - 1/2 cup of fast foods, coleslaw


 - 2 tablespoon of onions, raw


 - 1 tablespoon of vinegar, cider


 - 1/4 teaspoon of spices, pepper, red or cayenne


Instructions: 

Microwave asparagus, peas and water in microwaveable bowl on HIGH 1-1/2 to 2 min. or until asparagus is crisp-tender; drain. Rinse under cold water 1 min. ; drain well. Place in large bowl. Add cabbage; mix lightly. Mix remaining ingredients until blended. Add to cabbage mixture; toss to evenly coat.
