### Ingredients

 - 14 cup of alcoholic beverage, tequila sunrise, canned

 - 14 cup of sauce, worcestershire

 - 12 teaspoon of spices, pepper, black

 - 12 teaspoon of salt, table

 - 1 teaspoon of spices, garlic powder

### Instructions
