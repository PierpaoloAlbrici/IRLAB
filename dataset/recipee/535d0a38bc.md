## Dr. Oz. Energizing Breakfast: Canned Pumpkin Oatmeal

### Ingredients

 - 14 cup of pumpkin, raw

 - 1 cup of oats

 - 1 dash of spices, cinnamon, ground

### Instructions

Stir 1/4 cup canned pumpkin into 1 cup of prepared oatmeal. Sprinkle with cinnamon and enjoy!
