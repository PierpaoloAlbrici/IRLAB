Hot Bean Dip


Ingredients


 - 16 ounce of beans, snap, green, raw


 - 15 ounce of beans, snap, green, raw


 - 1/2 cup of cream, sour, cultured


 - 1 cup of sauce, salsa, ready-to-serve


 - 1 cup of cheese, cheddar


Instructions: 

Preheat oven to 375. Combine the beans & spread into an 8 square baking dish. Spread sour cream over the beans and top with the salsa and cheese. Bake for 20 30 minutes until bubbly. Serve with tortilla chips.
