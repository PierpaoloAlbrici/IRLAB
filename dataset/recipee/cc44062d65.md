## Marshmallow Sweet Potato Bake

[cc44062d65](http://www.food.com/recipe/marshmallow-sweet-potato-bake-73288)

### Ingredients

 - 2 1/2 pound of sweet potato, raw, unprepared

 - 14 cup of butter, without salt

 - 1/4-1/2 cup of sugars, granulated

 - 14 cup of raisins, seeded

 - 13 cup of nuts, walnuts, english

 - 13 cup of candies, marshmallows

### Instructions

Place sweet potatoes in a large saucepan, cover with water; bring to a boil for 30-35 mins, or just until tender, drain; cool slightly and peel In a large bowl, mash sweet potatoes, stir in butter, sugar and raisins Place in a greased 2-qt baking dish; sprinkle with nuts and marshmallows Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 mins or until heated through, and marshmallows start to brown.
