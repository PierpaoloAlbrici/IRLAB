### Ingredients

 - 3 tablespoon of rice, white, long-grain, regular, unenriched, cooked without salt

 - 1 tablespoon of sauce, salsa, ready-to-serve

 - 2 cup of lettuce, cos or romaine, raw

### Instructions

Mix all ingredients together and enjoy!
