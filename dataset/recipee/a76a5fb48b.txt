Creamy Oatmeal (Oats)


Ingredients


 - 1 cup of oats


 - 2 1/2 cup of water, bottled, generic


 - 1 cup of milk, fluid, 1% fat, without added vitamin a and vitamin d


 - 1 pinch of salt, table


Instructions: 

Mix all the ingredients in a pot Place on a hot plate and bring to the boil, stirring often When it starts boiling reduce the heat to medium and simmer uncovered for 8 minutes, stirring often Remove from heat and let it rest for 5 minutes Serve with butter and sugar or honey
