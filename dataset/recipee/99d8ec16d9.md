## Carla's Pineapple Honey Mustard Dressing

[99d8ec16d9](http://www.food.com/recipe/carlas-pineapple-honey-mustard-dressing-246694)

### Ingredients

 - 34 cup of salad dressing, mayonnaise, regular

 - 3 tablespoon of mustard, prepared, yellow

 - 2 tablespoon of honey

 - 2 tablespoon of pineapple juice, canned or bottled, unsweetened, without added ascorbic acid

 - 1 teaspoon of mustard, prepared, yellow

 - 1 tablespoon of vinegar, distilled

 - 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, raw

 - 1 dash of salt, table

### Instructions

Blend all ingredients together with a wisk. (Honey will mix in a better if it is slightly warm). Store in the refrigerator.
