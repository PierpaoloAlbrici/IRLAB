Salted Cashew Chocolate Bark


Ingredients


 - 1 cup of nuts, cashew nuts, raw


 - 6 ounce of pascha, organic bitter-sweet dark chocolate chips, upc: 842638005022


 - 6 ounce of spartan, real semi-sweet chocolate baking chips, upc: 011213162966


 - 1/4 teaspoon of salt, table


Instructions: 

Stir 1/2 cup nuts into each kind of chocolate. Drop spoonfuls of chocolate mixtures onto waxed paper-covered baking sheet, alternating colors of chocolates. Swirl with knife. Sprinkle with salt. Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm. Break into pieces.
