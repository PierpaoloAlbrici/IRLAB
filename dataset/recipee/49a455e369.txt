Lizzie's Favorite Peanut Butter Dip


Ingredients


 - 12 cup of peanut butter, smooth style, without salt


 - 12 cup of cream, whipped, cream topping, pressurized


 - 12 cup of cheese, parmesan, hard


Instructions: 

Mix all together in a medium sized bowl. Serve with pretzels, graham crackers, celery sticks, or spead on bread.
