## Bill Knapp's Honey-Ginger Chicken Marinade

[8b5aab063a](http://www.food.com/recipe/bill-knapps-honey-ginger-chicken-marinade-273088)

### Ingredients

 - 12 cup of honey

 - 12 cup of apple juice, canned or bottled, unsweetened, without added ascorbic acid

 - 2 teaspoon of spices, garlic powder

 - 12 teaspoon of spices, ginger, ground

 - 23 cup of soy sauce made from soy (tamari)

 - 12 cup of vinegar, cider

 - 23 cup of oil, olive, salad or cooking

### Instructions

Mix together all marinade ingredients in a blender. Pour into plastic zip-top bag with chicken; seal and let chicken marinate overnight in refrigerator. Let marinade drip off before cooking. Cook on grill, bake in oven or cook over medium-high heat in skillet on stove top. (Cooking time will vary on type of chicken parts and cooking method used. ).
