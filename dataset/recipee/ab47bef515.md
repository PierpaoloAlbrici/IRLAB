## Dilly Bar

[ab47bef515](https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/337182-dilly-bar)

### Ingredients

 - 1 ounce of milk, fluid, 1% fat, without added vitamin a and vitamin d

 - 1 ounce of chocolate, dark, 70-85% cacao solids

### Instructions

Shake both ingredients together with ice. Strain into a shot glass. Enjoy!
