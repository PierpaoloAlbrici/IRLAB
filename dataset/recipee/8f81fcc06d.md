## Cheese Potato Casserole

[8f81fcc06d](http://www.food.com/recipe/cheese-potato-casserole-16952)

### Ingredients

 - 4 cup of potatoes, raw, skin

 - 8 ounce of yogurt, greek, plain, nonfat

 - 1 cup of cheese, cottage, creamed, large or small curd

 - 12 teaspoon of salt, table

 - 14 teaspoon of spices, pepper, black

 - 14 teaspoon of dill weed, fresh

 - 1 cup of cheese, cheddar

### Instructions

Combine all ingredients except Cheddar cheese; spoon into a 9 inch baking pan. Top with cheddar cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
