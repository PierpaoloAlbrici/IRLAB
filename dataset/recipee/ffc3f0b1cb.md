## Buckeyes

[ffc3f0b1cb](http://www.food.com/recipe/buckeyes-5269)

### Ingredients

 - 1 cup of sugars, granulated

 - 12 cup of peanut butter, smooth style, without salt

 - 3 tablespoon of butter, without salt

 - 1 pound of spartan, real semi-sweet chocolate baking chips, upc: 011213162966

### Instructions

Combine powdered sugar, peanut butter and butter. Roll into 1 inch balls and chill until firm. Dip into melted chocolate and place on waxed paper until hard.
