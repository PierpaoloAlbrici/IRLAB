Simple Glaze


Ingredients


 - 1/4 cup of mustard, prepared, yellow


 - 13 cup of sugars, brown


 - 2 tablespoon of orange juice, raw


Instructions: 

Mix 1/4 cup mustard, 1/3 cup brown sugar, and 2 tablespoons orange juice. Brush on the ham and bake at 350 degrees F, basting occasionally until warmed through.
