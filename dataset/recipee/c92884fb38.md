## Easiest Ice Cream Ever!

[c92884fb38](https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/343133-easiest-ice-cream-ever)

### Ingredients

 - 600 ml of cream, whipped, cream topping, pressurized

 - 400 ml of milk, canned, condensed, sweetened

### Instructions

Beat the whipping cream until it form soft peaks Fold in the condensed milk You can add anything you like into the ice cream(example:Avocado,chocolate,banana) Pour it into a container and let it freeze in the freezer
