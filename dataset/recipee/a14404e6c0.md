## Bisquick Mix IIi

[a14404e6c0](http://www.food.com/recipe/bisquick-mix-iii-23982)

### Ingredients

 - 9 cup of wheat flour, white, all-purpose, unenriched

 - 5 tablespoon of leavening agents, baking powder, double-acting, sodium aluminum sulfate

 - 1 tablespoon of salt, table

 - 2 teaspoon of cream, whipped, cream topping, pressurized

 - 1 3/4 cup of shortening, vegetable, household, composite

### Instructions

Sift dry ingredients together. Using electric mixer, blend in shortening until mixture feels like corn meal. Store in airtight container. Use baking mix for biscuits, pancakes, etc. Try using a mixture of 1/2 water and 1/2 plain yogurt w/ dash of lemon juice to make great biscuits!
